feat(demo): Station 7 'free digital book' downloads the REAL PDF (not a stub)

The AssembledBook PDF/ePub blobs were never carried Station 6 -> Station 7
(Station 6 persisted only metadata), so 'Get the free digital book' emitted a
.txt stub with the shortcode/hash. Added an in-memory generatedBookStore
(stores.ts) that Station 6 fills with the real { pdfBlob, epubBlob, tree,
shortcode, title } after the pipeline succeeds; Station 7 downloadDigital()
now downloads the actual print-ready PDF, falling back to the stub only if the
blob was lost (reload mid-flow).

BROWSER-VERIFIED: download is storybook-<shortcode>.pdf, application/pdf,
161,664 bytes (matches the ~157.9 KB seal-card size).
