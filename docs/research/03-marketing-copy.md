## 2. Feature Blurbs

### ✨ Read-along that lights up the words
Each word gently highlights in time with the narrator's voice, so your child begins to feel how the squiggles on the page connect to the sounds in their ear — a quiet superpower called orthographic mapping, and the single biggest predictor of reading fluency.

### 🔤 Tap any word to sound it out
Stuck on "dragon" or new to "whisper"? A single tap breaks the word into its sounds using systematic synthetic phonics — the same step-by-step decoding method used in the world's highest-performing classrooms.

### 🦄 Your child is the hero of every story
Their name, their face, their choices — woven into the adventure from the dedication page to the final line. Personalization isn't just a delight; it's a proven engagement engine, and it turns a passive listener into a deeply invested reader.

## 3. The Science Behind the Magic

- **Sounds are taught in order, blended carefully, never rushed.** Every word is decoded the way expert readers do it — left to right, sound by sound. *(Systematic synthetic phonics)*
- **Words are mapped to memory, not memorized as whole shapes.** Highlighting, pacing, and repetition help your child bind letters to sounds the way skilled readers' brains do automatically. *(Ehri's orthographic mapping)*
- **We talk *with* your child about the story, not *at* them.** Gentle prompts invite them to predict, picture, and retell — the same back-and-forth that supercharges vocabulary and comprehension. *(Dialogic reading — the PEER + CROWD framework)*
- **Stories are sprinkled with rich, useful words children rarely hear in everyday talk.** "Enormous" instead of "big." "Trembling" instead of "shaky." These are the words that move the needle on reading comprehension. *(Tier-2 vocabulary)*
- **Words, pictures, and sound are designed to work together, not fight for attention.** Nothing distracts, nothing competes — so your child's brain builds stronger reading pathways with every page. *(Mayer's principles of multimedia learning, woven into Scarborough's Reading Rope)*
